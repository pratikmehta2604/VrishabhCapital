About Vrishabh Capital

At Vrishabh Capital, we blend financial intellect with disciplined execution.

Founded by Pratik Mehta, a Chartered Accountant with over a decade of experience in proprietary trading, market arbitrage, and equity research, the firm is built on a foundation of insight-led strategies and a commitment to integrity.

After a successful tenure at one of Ahmedabad’s leading hedge funds, Pratik set out to build an independent proprietary trading desk — with the vision to scale precision-driven strategies and maintain full control over risk and research. Since inception, Vrishabh Capital has consistently delivered returns north of 15%, with a focus on long-term sustainability and adaptability.

We specialize in:

  • Cross-market arbitrage: Leveraging inefficiencies across global markets.
  • Exchange arbitrage (NSE/BSE): Capturing short-term dislocations in Indian equities.
  • Trend-following strategies: Systematic, yet flexible — combining data models with market feel.
  • Discretionary trading: Backed by years of experience and grounded research.

Our approach is rooted in discipline, market structure awareness, and a relentless focus on risk-adjusted performance.

What We Do

At Vrishabh Capital, we operate at the intersection of structure and intuition — blending systematic models with real-time market experience. Our strategies are focused on efficiency, liquidity, and momentum, allowing us to stay agile and opportunistic in changing market conditions.

Cross-Market Arbitrage

We identify and exploit inefficiencies across exchanges and geographies. Backed by custom execution systems, we act quickly when price and time are misaligned.

Exchange Arbitrage (NSE/BSE)

We run intra-exchange arbitrage strategies in Indian equity markets, with a focus on liquidity, latency, and precision. These trades are executed via automated systems.

Trend-Following Strategies

We monitor short- and medium-term momentum trends using a combination of rule-based filters and discretionary overlays. Charts, price action, and event context guide our entries and exits.

Discretionary Trading

Some setups require a human edge. Discretionary trades are shaped by market intuition, macro context, and ongoing technical validation.

Our primary focus is in the futures segment, though we are steadily building toward swing-based equity strategies to diversify over time.
